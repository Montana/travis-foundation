Let's make Open Source an even better place!

How?

With your help we will support non-profit projects that make our community an even better place. Here are some projects that we are planning to start with:

  • RailsGirls Summer of Code

    Rails Girls students went Open Source. Starting July 2013 students worked fulltime for 3 months on great projects. Read more …

  • Open Source Funds

    Help selected Open Source projects kickstart or continue by supporting developers and organize funding. Read more …

  • Diversity in Tech

    Resources that help fostering diversity, and raise awareness of diversity issues within our community.

What is this?

Travis Foundation is a work-in-progress non-profit initiative aiming to give back and pay forward to the Open Source community.

Travis CI has always been very much rooted in Open Source. Working with the community to build a service for the community has been one wild love and succes story. Since Travis CI now is an established, solid service, it is time to give back even more.

With Travis Foundation we will use our expertise to support projects that provide opportunities to newcomers, foster diversity and make the community an even greater place in general.
