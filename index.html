What is this?

Travis Foundation is a work-in-progress non-profit initiative aiming to give back and pay forward to the Open Source community.

Travis CI has always been very much rooted in Open Source. Working with the community to build a service for the community has been one wild love and succes story. Since Travis CI now is an established, solid service, it is time to give back even more.

With Travis Foundation we will use our expertise to support projects that provide opportunities to newcomers, foster diversity and make the community an even greater place in general.
